Physical edition

Paperbacks and hardcovers

The campaign will start with a digital-first release. Paperback and hardcover editions will only be unlocked if funding covers production, printing, and shipping in a sustainable way [web:103][page:1].

Digital first

The first edition of A Long Day will be released digitally. This keeps the campaign realistic and focused while the book is being finished, edited, and prepared for release.

Printed editions if funding allows

If the campaign raises enough to cover manufacturing and fulfillment, we will offer a physical edition. The exact format and price will depend on the final costs for paperback and hardcover production, packaging, and shipping.

Why this matters

This approach keeps expectations clear: backers know the book will definitely exist in digital form, and any printed version will be a funded stretch beyond that core release.
